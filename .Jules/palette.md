## 2026-04-06 - Focus States and Deep Linking for Tags\n**Learning:** Anchor links generated dynamically on the client require corresponding `id` attributes on their targets, and those targets need scroll margin (e.g. `scroll-mt-24`) to avoid being hidden under fixed headers. Also, explicit `focus-visible` utility classes greatly improve keyboard navigation for dynamic client-side links.\n**Action:** Always test deep links and apply scroll margins to anchor targets; consistently apply explicit focus styles to all interactive elements.
