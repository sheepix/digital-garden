## 2024-03-09 - Semantic Navigation and Accessible Deep Links for Tags
**Learning:** Using simple `div` wrappers for interactive groups of links (like tags) misses an opportunity to provide context to screen reader users. Additionally, deep links targeting dynamically generated elements often land behind fixed headers unless given appropriate scroll margins (`scroll-mt-*`), and require proper URL encoding (`encodeURIComponent`) to handle special characters in tags safely. Focus states are frequently overlooked in dynamically injected HTML but are critical for keyboard accessibility.
**Action:** Always wrap tag collections in semantic `<nav aria-label="...">` elements, apply `focus-visible` states consistently even in vanilla JS rendering, and ensure anchor targets have `encodeURIComponent` + `scroll-mt` offsets to prevent them from being hidden behind sticky UI elements.
